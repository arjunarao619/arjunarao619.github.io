Arjun Rao

I am a third-year undergraduate student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong majoring in Financial Technology and Data Analytics.

I am interested in adversarial robustness in deep learning with a focus on how natural or adversarial data shifts can affect generalization and loss landscapes. I am also interested in studying how robustness can create better representations in neural networks.

I am currently working on distributed/decentralized optimization theory under the supervision of Prof. Hoi To Wai.
